How to Play

You are the ADAFCO, operating from the C2 TOC Callsign "Bigfoot", coordinating with the TD in the ICC Callsign "Guardian". Your mission is to execute a 360° Point-Defense operation: prevent fratricide, manage track engagement, and prosecute hostile air threats as directed by the SL. Success requires speed, judgment, and decisive task prioritization.

Mission Flow

  1. For best training value: All internal calls to the Crew and the SL should be simulated.
  2. COVER Phase
    The Senior Director (SL) calls: “COVER Track 12345, B/E 123/45.”
    • Left-click the track.
    • Evaluate geometry & ID, then press and hold [Ctrl] to activate radio P/T.
    • Voice to ICC "Guardian, Bigfoot, Cover Track 12345, 27 Thousand, Hostile" or "Cover Group Bullseye 123/45"...
    • Use the appropriate hotkey to notify the Crew/SL once Birds Affirm or Birds Negate. A (AFFIRM) or N (NEGATE).
  3. AFFIRM → ENGAGE
    • The SL will order ENGAGE automatically or after your AFFIRM.
    • When the SL gives an Engage order, the track will be tagged ENGAGE.
    • Voice to ICC "Guardian, Bigfoot, Engage Track 12345, 27 Thousand, Hostile" or "...Engage Group Bullseye 123/45"...
    • Two missiles are launched by ICC from the nearest in-geometry Firing Unit. • Press W (AWAY) to notify the Crew/SL once ICC voices "Birds Away" on a track.
  4. VANISH / HOLD FIRE
    Hits remove the track automatically. To abort an intercept and prevent fraticide, voice to ICC "Guardian, Bigfoot, Hold Fire track 12345" (engaged track).
    or pressH (HOLD FIRE) with the engaged track selected.

Action Bar (bottom-right)

  • REDWOOD – arms “weapons-hot” in a quadrant
    • When REDWOOD HOT is active (red) ADAFCO holds engagment authority in that quadrant.
    • • Voice to SL "SL, ADAFCO, request REDWOOD HOT, NW Quadrant • SL will assess for a few seconds and Respond with "Approved" or "Denied" based on number of friendlies and number of hostiles. • You can also request nonverbally by tapping REDWOOD → choose NW NE SW SE.
    • when activated, no ROE penalties in that sector until the SL deactivates it.
  • FIREBALL – global TBM defense switch
    • Arms Patriots (50 NM gate) and NASAMS (20 NM gate) for all incoming TBM waves.
    • • Activated by voicing "Crew FIREBALL FIREBALL FIREBALL" or "Crew, FIREBALL NW Quadrant."
    • You can also activatie nonverbally by tapping FIREBALL.
  • State Checks – You may request "What State" from "Guardian" at any time.
    • Weapons Green More than 50% interceptors remaining per FU.
    • Weapons Yellow 25%-50% interceptors remaining per FU.
    • Weapons Red Less than 25% interceptors remaining per FU.

Scoring

  • +1 Valid missile kill
  • +2 FIREBALL intercept
  • –0.5 Contract Violation (communicate engagement status via [A] and [W] Hotkeys)
  • –1 Birds/shots trashed (miss if no geometry)
  • –5 ROE violation (engaged without authority)
  • –6 Friendly fire (miss)
  • –20 Fratricide
  • –5 TBM craters island
  • –7 ABT damages an asset

Mission Success & Failure

Success: You win when you successfully defend the island from a full air attack level and maintain a positive score.
Failure: You lose if DAL/assets are impacted 5 times, or 5 contract/ROE violations occur.
The mission ends immediately if:

  • Friendly Fire / Fratricide – an interceptor kills a friendly track

    Threat Types

    • Hostile aircraft – maneuver, drop precision bombs at 10 NM & 5 NM. Heavy groups start at Level 3+
    • TBM waves – groups of 1 to 3 ballistic missiles start at Level 3+. Use FIREBALL to prime auto-engage.
    • Cruise missiles – low-alt clustered shots start at Level 2+.
    • Friendlies – Returning to the Island on Procedural MRR. Descend from 20 NM / 200 MSL; IDENT, Do not engage!

    Levels & Progression

    1. Level 1 – 10 hostiles.
    2. Level 2 – +5 hostiles, adds cruise-missile raid.
    3. Level 3 – TBM wave ‎(+ FIREBALL).
    4. Level 4+ – Random radar outages.
